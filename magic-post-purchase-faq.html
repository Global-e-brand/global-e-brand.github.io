Magic Post Purchase Upsell FAQ

Q1: What are Product Page Upsells and how do they work? A1: Product Page Upsells are designed to show highly relevant add-ons and bundles directly on the product page. This allows shoppers to see and consider additional items right at the point where they are making their purchasing decisions.

Q2: How do Checkout & Post-Purchase Upsells help increase Average Order Value (AOV)? A2: These upsells increase AOV by presenting frictionless, one-click offers to customers both during and immediately after the checkout process, encouraging them to add more items to their purchase with ease.

Q3: What kind of offers are available during and after checkout? A3: During and after checkout, customers are presented with frictionless one-click offers, making it easy for them to add additional products to their order and thereby increasing the overall value of their purchase.

Q4: What are Thank You Page Offers and how do they generate revenue? A4: Thank You Page Offers turn order confirmation pages into revenue opportunities. They provide last-chance, personalized deals to customers after their purchase is complete, maximizing sales even after checkout.
