Magic Cart Min & Max: Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Magic Cart Min & Max? Magic Cart Min & Max is a new feature that allows you to set minimum and maximum quantity limits for individual products in your store. This gives you greater control over how much of a specific item a customer can purchase in a single order.

2. Why should I use Magic Cart Min & Max? This feature is designed to help you manage your inventory and customer experience. It's particularly useful if you want to prevent customers from purchasing products in bulk for reselling, ensuring more customers have access to popular items. Conversely, if you have an overstock of a product, you can use it to encourage larger purchases to clear inventory faster.

3. How do I set quantity limits for my products? You can easily choose specific products and assign a minimum and maximum quantity directly within your store's settings. This allows for flexible control over each item.

4. Do I need any technical skills or developer assistance to use this feature? No, absolutely not! There's no need to install any extra code or seek developer assistance. Magic Cart Min & Max is designed to be user-friendly and can be set up easily.

5. Can I customize the appearance of the quantity limit messages or popups? Yes, you can customize the content and design of any related popups or messages to match your store's existing theme and branding.
