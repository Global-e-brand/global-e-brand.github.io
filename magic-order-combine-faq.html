Magic Order Combine FAQ

Q1: What is Magic Order Combine? A1: Magic Order Combine is a tool designed to automatically merge similar orders with a single click of a button, helping store owners streamline their order management process.

Q2: How does Magic Order Combine simplify my order process? A2: If you're overwhelmed by a high volume of orders, Magic Order Combine simplifies things by automatically merging similar orders. This helps streamline the shipping process and frees up your time for more important tasks.

Q3: What are the main features of Magic Order Combine? A3: Key features include automatic order combining, the ability to select a specific date range for orders you wish to merge, a record of all combined orders, and dedicated email support.

Q4: How do I use Magic Order Combine? A4: All you have to do is select the date range for the orders you wish to merge. Magic Order Combine will then automatically identify and combine similar orders within that period.

Q5: What are the benefits of using Magic Order Combine for my store? A5: By automatically combining similar orders, Magic Order Combine helps you streamline your shipping process, save time, and reduce the feeling of being overwhelmed by a large number of orders, allowing you to allocate your time more efficiently.
